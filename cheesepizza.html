Cheese Pizza

Cheese pizza is one of the most popular meals in the world because of its simple ingredients and delicious taste. The pizza dough is prepared using flour, yeast, water, and salt, which gives the base a soft and chewy texture when baked properly.

The tomato sauce is made from fresh tomatoes and herbs, providing a rich flavor. Mozzarella cheese melts evenly and creates the stretchy texture everyone loves. Baking at high temperature ensures a crispy crust.

Cheese pizza can be eaten alone or with extra toppings. It is perfect for family meals, parties, and casual dining.
