Tuo Zaafi (TZ)

Tuo Zaafi, commonly called TZ, is a traditional dish from Northern Ghana. It is made from maize or millet flour cooked into a thick paste. TZ is usually served with green leafy soup prepared with ayoyo or okra.

The soup is rich and nutritious, often containing dried fish or meat. Tuo Zaafi is soft and smooth, making it easy to swallow. It is eaten by forming small balls and dipping them into the soup.

Tuo Zaafi is a staple meal in Northern Ghana and is valued for its nutritional benefits and cultural importance.
