Ghana Jollof Rice

Ghana Jollof Rice is one of the most popular and beloved dishes in Ghana and across West Africa. It is well known for its rich tomato flavor, attractive orange-red color, and delicious aroma. Ghana Jollof Rice is commonly prepared for special occasions such as weddings, parties, festivals, and family gatherings. It is also enjoyed as an everyday meal because of its satisfying taste and filling nature.

The preparation of Ghana Jollof Rice begins with making a flavorful tomato sauce. Fresh tomatoes, onions, peppers, and spices are blended and cooked in oil until the sauce becomes thick and rich. Seasonings such as salt, ginger, garlic, curry powder, and thyme are added to enhance the flavor. Meat, chicken, or fish can be cooked in the sauce to add extra taste before the rice is added.

Washed rice is then poured into the sauce and stirred gently to ensure that every grain is coated with the tomato mixture. Water or stock is added, and the rice is allowed to cook slowly until it becomes soft and well-seasoned. Ghana Jollof Rice is often served with fried chicken, grilled meat, salad, or boiled eggs. Its rich taste, smoky aroma, and vibrant color make it a favorite dish enjoyed by people of all ages.
